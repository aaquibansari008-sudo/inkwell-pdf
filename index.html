Privacy

Why nothing gets uploaded

Inkwell does all its work locally, using your browser's own PDF engine. Your document is opened, edited and saved again without a single byte going to a server.

That means no waiting on an upload, no privacy policy to read before you trust it with a lease or a tax form, and it keeps working even on a shaky connection.

FAQ

Good to know

Is Inkwell really free?

Yes. There's no subscription, no watermark, and no limit on how many PDFs you fill out or sign.

Do I need to create an account?

No. There's no sign-up screen, no email address, and nothing to remember a password for.

Is my document uploaded anywhere?

No. Everything happens in your browser using your device's own processing — your file never leaves your computer or phone.

Can I use it on my phone?

Yes. The toolbar and page layout adjust for smaller screens, and every box can be dragged and resized with a finger.

What about scanned documents?

Inkwell overlays text, ticks, and signatures on top of the page — it doesn't read handwriting on a scan, so just make sure your additions land over blank space.
